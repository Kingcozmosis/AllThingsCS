Commands

What the bot can do

```

/player

Search for CS2 pro players with support for interactive selection.

/team

Show a CS2 team roster and team information.

/vs

Compare two players with color-coded stats and winner highlights.

/matches

See today’s or live Counter-Strike matches.

/tournaments

Track live tournaments and competitive events.

/callouts

View map callouts and important positions.

/instants

View CS2 smoke lineups quickly in Discord.

/help

View all available commands and bot features.

```
